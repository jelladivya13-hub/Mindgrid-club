About Our Director

Dr. Sanjeev Shrivastava is a distinguished academic leader with a strong vision for holistic education and student-centered learning. With extensive experience in academic leadership, administration, and mentoring, he has played a vital role in shaping institutions that focus on excellence, discipline, and innovation.

He believes that true education is not limited to examinations, but lies in developing critical thinking, creativity, and ethical values among students. His approach emphasizes learning by doing, collaboration, and continuous improvement. He constantly motivates students to explore their potential and prepares them to face real-world challenges with confidence.

Under his dynamic leadership, initiatives like Mind Grid Club have been introduced to provide students with a platform beyond the classroom. These initiatives encourage technical skills, problem-solving abilities, teamwork, and leadership qualities. He strongly supports student-driven activities, workshops, projects, and interactive learning sessions.
